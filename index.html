licht en leven.

Casa Nilaya is een vrijstaande bungalow met drie slaapkamers, twee badkamers en een ruime garage — gebouwd op een kavel van circa 2.000 m² op het zuiden.

luz e vida.

Casa Nilaya é um bungalow isolado com três quartos, duas casas de banho e uma ampla garagem — construído num lote de 2.000 m² orientado a sul.

Grandes janelas deixam entrar luz e paisagem. Uma piscina privada, terraços generosos e um jardim que se funde com as colinas.

Space for rest,
light and life.

Casa Nilaya is a detached bungalow with three bedrooms, two bathrooms and a spacious garage — set on a 2,000 m² south-facing plot.

Large glass panels allow light and landscape to flow inside. A private pool, generous terraces and a garden that merges with the hills.
